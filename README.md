 # PROJECT

My first project during Second Semester is Idiyanale NPK Sensor, Under the gudance of Sir Kodic De Guzman and Sir Julius Faustino.
This project was succesfully preseted during our school exhibit, allowing us to showcase our programming, hardware integration and teamwork skills.

The NPK Sensor is a Smart Agricultural System designed to measure the level of nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K) present in the soil.
Thesse essential play a vital role in the growth by supporting left development, root strength, and overall crop productivity.

The project uses an NPK sensor and Arduino Uno to monitor soil nutrient levels in real time. It helps farmers and gardeners to determine the right
fertilizer amount, reducing waste and improving crop growth through efficient soil monitoring.
